Recipe for the perfect suunny side egg

Ingredients

For a half cooked

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper

For a crisp texture

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat the butter in a pan over medium heat
  2. Crack the egg into the pan
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Cook for 2 minutes for a half cooked egg
  5. Cook for 4 minutes for a crisp texture
  6. Remove from the pan and serve
